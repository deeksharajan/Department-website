About Our Department

The Department of Computer Technology was established with the aim of producing skilled engineers capable of contributing to the rapidly evolving world of technology. Our department has consistently been a hub for innovation, research, and excellence in technical education.

We focus on empowering students with a strong foundation in computer science concepts, hands-on programming experience, and the latest industry practices. The department boasts a team of dedicated faculty members who blend academic rigor with real-world applications.

Our Mission

To provide quality education and foster an environment of research, innovation, and ethical practices that prepares students to become global technology leaders and problem solvers.

Our Vision

To emerge as a center of excellence in computer science education and research, producing graduates who will drive the technological future of the nation.

Our Objectives

  • To impart industry-oriented technical knowledge and skills.
  • To encourage innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship among students.
  • To establish collaborations with leading industries and research organizations.
  • To instill professional ethics and lifelong learning habits.
